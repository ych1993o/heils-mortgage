About Us / 关于我们

Mortgage Calculator is a free online tool designed to help homebuyers, homeowners, and real estate professionals calculate mortgage payments quickly and accurately.

房贷计算器是一款免费的在线工具，旨在帮助购房者、房主和房地产专业人士快速、准确地计算房贷还款额。

Our Mission / 我们的使命

We believe that understanding your mortgage payments shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to provide a simple, accurate, and accessible mortgage calculation tool that helps people make informed financial decisions about their home purchases.

我们相信，理解房贷还款不应复杂。我们的使命是提供简单、准确、易用的房贷计算工具，帮助人们做出明智的购房财务决策。

What We Offer / 我们提供的服务

Our mortgage calculator supports two common repayment methods: equal payment (equal installments) and equal principal. We also provide a detailed amortization schedule, allowing you to see exactly how each payment is split between principal and interest over the life of your loan.

我们的房贷计算器支持两种常见还款方式：等额本息和等额本金。我们还提供详细的还款明细表，让您清楚地看到整个贷款期内每期还款中本金和利息的分配情况。
